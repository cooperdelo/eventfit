# About

### 2️⃣ About Page (`/about`)

**Goal:** Tell the brand story + introduce mission and team.

### Structure:

1. **Hero Section**
   - Simple headline: “Making Campus Style Effortless.”
   - Subtext about sustainability, confidence, and community.
   - Illustration: campus skyline or people collage.
2. **Mission**
   - Two-column layout: left text, right image of diverse students.
   - “EventFit was built to make finding what to wear stress-free and fun.”
3. **Our Story**
   - Timeline (Lucide `clock` or `flag` icon).
   - Points: concept origin, pilot schools, what’s next.
4. **Team Section**
   - Card layout of founders:
     - Name, title, short blurb, school, social icon links.
     - Keep it authentic, not corporate.
     - Lucide icons: `linkedin`, `mail`.
5. **Join Us CTA**
   - Text: “We’re just getting started. Want to help bring EventFit to your campus?”
   - Button: “Get Involved” → email link.
